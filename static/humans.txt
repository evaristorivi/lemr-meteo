/* PURPOSE */
Web application for real-time meteorological information specifically designed for ultralight pilots operating from La Morgal aerodrome (LEMR) in Asturias, Spain. Combines official AEMET forecasts, real-time METAR data, wind maps, and AI-powered analysis for better flight decisions.
